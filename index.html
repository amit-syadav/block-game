rules

Welcome, to the Block Game.
It is a single player game.
Your aim is to maximize your score by arranging blocks in same level.
When a level of block is completed it vanished, creating space for new blocks.
For each level you will get a score of +10.
The game ends when there is no space for new blocks.
Please use the UP key to rotate your block, and LEFT and RIGHT to
move across the board. Pressing DOWN will speed up the block.
See you inside !
